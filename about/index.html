About

Hey Hello I'm Jamil. When I was 8 I built a massively tiny tree fortress in my backyard named Area 52. No girls allowed. My life since has been a metaphorical reenactment of that project. Well, except for the no girls allowed part (still working on that).

I recently took a sabbatical from software contracting in the Bay Area to build a tiny house. This blog is an attempt to document the progress.
